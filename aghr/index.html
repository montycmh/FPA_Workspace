What this workspace does
  • Reads the monthly Workday extract directly in the browser.
  • Applies the AGHR business rules for worker-type normalization, hire-date filtering, and FTE versus contingent classification.
  • Builds KPI cards, movement views, raw-data tabs, and Pigment output.
  • Generates one self-contained HTML report you can download or share.
Supported file pattern
  • AGHRMmmYY.xlsx
  • Example: AGHRJul26.xlsx
  • Report month comes from the filename.
  • Effective month is the month before the filename month.
