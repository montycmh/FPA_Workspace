What this workspace does
  • Reads the monthly HC Actuals workbook directly in the browser.
  • Maps classes to business units using the workbook’s Mapping tab.
  • Builds the function-level table with hires, attrition, transfers, subtotals, and total net headcount.
  • Creates a self-contained report you can share or paste into slides.
Supported file patterns

  • Requires two tabs: sheet1 and Mapping
  • Costa Rica stays editable in the generated report.
