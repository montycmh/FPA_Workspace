What this workspace does
  • Reads only the Cost center tab from each SBC forecast workbook.
  • Maps each cost center to its ID and business name before export.
  • Validates exported totals against the source workbook total.
  • Builds quarter CSVs and one consolidated feed for Pigment or downstream use.
Working rules
  • Zero-value rows are dropped; negative values are kept.
  • Unmapped rows block CSV export until they are resolved.
  • Mapping edits are saved locally in your browser for this workspace.
  • Fiscal quarter dates are translated to the last month of the quarter.
